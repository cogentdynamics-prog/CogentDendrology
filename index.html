Cogent Dendrology

Engineering curiosity applied to forests.

Cogent Dendrology is a nonprofit educational project dedicated to documenting trees, forests, and stewardship through careful observation, measurement, photography, and long-term learning.

Our mission is to inspire curiosity, encourage responsible stewardship, and build a freely available resource that helps people better understand the remarkable lives of trees.

Every tree has a story. Our job is simply to help tell it.
